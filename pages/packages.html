Packages and Pricing

I am currently taking new referrals for telehealth counseling, and traveling equine-assisted psychotherapy. I am able to accommodate multiple payment options.

I am currently able to accept UnitedHealthCare insurance as well as Oxford and Oscar plans.

For clients who either have other insurance coverage, who are under-insured or who do not have insurance I have a sliding scale for these services. The sliding scales allow clients to pay whatever amount they are able to each session. I encourage clients to contact their insurance companies to explore the possibility of reimbursement or having this out-of-pocket expense count toward deductibles. Payment is expected at the time of service.

  • Telehealth Counseling: $60 to $120 per session
  • Traveling Equine-Assisted Psychotherapy: $80 to $160 per session plus additional fee for travel

To set up your first session email Jordan at jordan@cusumanolmsw.com

Late Cancellation and No Show Policy

Sometimes life happens and we have to cancel or we forget completely that we have committed to a session. Always reach out to Jordan as soon as you are aware of the conflict. If you are able to notify Jordan 24 hours ahead of appointment there is no penalty and rescheduling will be navigated. Should you need to cancel with less than 24 hours notice you will be charged up to the full fee of your session.
