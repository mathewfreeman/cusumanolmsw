Things to Remember Before Your First Session



I hope you are as excited as I am that you are preparing for your first Equine-Assisted Psychotherapy session! Here are some reminders as you prepare for your first session.

  • Check the weather and dress accordingly! Sessions will be primarily held outside and going "inside" will just mean that we are in the barn or arena where it's 'warmer' but not heated. Layering is useful.
  • Wear clothes that you are comfortable getting dirty - we don't expect you to get particularly dirty but it can be distracting for clients to be worrying about that during sessions.
  • Wear closed-toed shoes that can get muddy/dirty. We recomment boots if possible, but we won't be able to hold your session if you wear sandals!
  • Complete the onboarding paperwork that Jordan will send you via the 'portal' at least 24 hours before your first session. If it's not completed in time we will have to reschedule.
  • Bring an open mind and an open heart!
  • Bring your mask and be prepared to wear it during your session in instances where social distancing is not possible.
